Stackalytics

  • Provides transparent and meaningful statistics regarding contributions to both OpenStack itself and projects related to OpenStack
    • Transparency: the community can have confidence that all calculations are correct and fair
    • Meaningful: anyone may submit a correction that will adjust the influence of appropriate statistical data
  • Collects and processes development activity data and makes it possible to visualize it in a convenient web dashboard
  • http://stackalytics.com

Note

  • Stackalytics Wiki: https://wiki.openstack.org/wiki/Stackalytics
  • Stackalytics site is being migrated to http://stackalytics.openstack.org by OpenStack Infrastructure team.
